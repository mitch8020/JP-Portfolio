About Me

I'm a software developer based in Nashville, TN!


I started my professional career by learning 3D modeling design by myself. As a result of that, I had the opportunity to work as a senior designer for 2+ years, where I discovered my passion for design and the ability to materialize every customer's ideas. After that, due to COVID-19, I transitioned into my career as a customer service specialist, providing fast and efficient solutions to my customers.


Being always passionate about software engineering, I decided to transition to web development to start a new phase, taking advantage of all my designing and customer service experience to provide creative, efficient, and characteristic solutions and experiences.


When I'm not coding, you can find me playing music, working out, gaming or reading articles about space and science.

Combine leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving skills to provide high-quality work in conjunction with applied analytical skills. Known as a persistent, patient, and hard-working student willing to tackle difficult projects with outstanding and consistent results. Able to efficiently complete tasks on a timely manner and show dependable progress. Background includes residential construction development and management, roadway and bridge construction, CAD and Microstation, and recruiting.
